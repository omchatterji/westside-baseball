About Westside

Development With Intent.

Westside Baseball is built around strong instruction, efficient training plans, and a competitive team culture that pushes players forward.

What We Offer

Our program combines high-energy clinics, position-specific skill work, private and small-group instruction, and travel team opportunities. The goal is simple: create a training environment where players improve quickly and understand how to transfer that work into games.

  • Clinic sessions built around hitting, defense, baserunning, and throwing progression.
  • Training plans that scale from beginner fundamentals to high-level competitive preparation.
  • Instruction that emphasizes repeatable mechanics, baseball IQ, and confidence under pressure.

Program Identity

Clinics Travel Teams Private Instruction Offseason Work High School Prep
  • Approach: Competitive reps with clear coaching feedback.
  • Focus: Athletic development, discipline, and team-first habits.
  • Environment: Modern, fast-moving, and demanding in the right ways.
