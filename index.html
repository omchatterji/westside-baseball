About Westside

Development With Intent.

Westside Baseball is built around strong instruction, efficient training plans, and a competitive team culture that pushes players forward.

What We Offer

Our program combines high-energy clinics, position-specific skill work, private and small-group instruction, and travel team opportunities. The goal is simple: create a training environment where players improve quickly and understand how to transfer that work into games.

  • Clinic sessions built around hitting, defense, baserunning, and throwing progression.
  • Training plans that scale from beginner fundamentals to high-level competitive preparation.
  • Instruction that emphasizes repeatable mechanics, baseball IQ, and confidence under pressure.

Program Identity

Clinics Travel Teams Private Instruction Offseason Work High School Prep
  • Approach: Competitive reps with clear coaching feedback.
  • Focus: Athletic development, discipline, and team-first habits.
  • Environment: Modern, fast-moving, and demanding in the right ways.

Coaches

Instruction Led By Coaches Who Teach The Details.

Brooks Graff

Director & Program Head

Brooks leads Westside Baseball as Director & Program Head. He has also been the head baseball coach at Parker High School since the fall of 2024.

Originally from Green Bay, Brooks is a 2001 graduate of Notre Dame Academy where he was a High School All-State Player. He went on to both play and coach at the collegiate level:
2002 - Ohio State University
2003 - Gulf Coast Community College
2004-2005 - University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee
2006 - Graduate Assistant Coach at University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee

After moving to Janesville in 2010, Brooks started his own baseball & softball training business, partnering with youth travel programs such as GRB Academy and Thundercat Baseball to teach lessons at their facilities as well as providing lessons locally at the cages above MMPR.

Over the years, Brooks has worked with hundreds of players who have gone on to play in college and professionally. He has trained and mentored several players who have gone on to play in the MLB, including Danny Jansen, Ben Rortvedt, Drake Baldwin, Jake Campbell, Jack Blomgren, and Alan Roden. Today, he hopes to provide that same caliber of coaching to aspiring players on Janesville's westside.

"My focus now is to grow the game on the westside of Janesville. To provide a quality summer program for our high school players in Janesville. And, to provide training opportunities for youth players 8th grade and under!"
